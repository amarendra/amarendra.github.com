Colophon

I wanted a simple yet functional layout and I didn't want to piece everything together myself. I had also decided tp migrate this to a statis site generator. I found Jekyll to be very good. Though I resisted for long as I wanted something based on Python, the language I know little bit and wish to continue learning further. But the absence of any really good python-based alternatives and if at all there are few then the documentation, resources and themes available for them leave a lot to be desired. Besides, setting up Jekyll is so easy and straight forward. You should try it if you are looking for something similar.
