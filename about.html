About Me

For high school I moved to a boarding school called SSVM near Rosera where I had best of my years.

Then I moved to Patna for undergraduate studies in the field of Computer Science.

After graduating I moved to Bangalore and worked at Samsung's India mobile R&D division SRI-B for a few years. Then at Ola Cabs. Currently (as of Jan 2024) I am working at Socure. Domain wise, over the years, it's been all over (it's kinda fun, building things in a new domain) but tech has been Android and mobile pretty much throughout.
