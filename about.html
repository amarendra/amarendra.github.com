About Me

I was born and brought up in the village of Ijraha (in the district of Begusarai, state Bihar in northern India) where I finished primary school. Then, for high-school(secondary) I moved to a boarding school SSVM near Rosera (map link) where I had best of my years.

I did my senior-secondary/intermediate at Chinmaya Vidyalaya Bokaro before moving to Patna for undergraduate studies in the field of Computer Science. I was awarded the degree of Bachelor of Technology by the department of Information Technology at NIT Patna in 2010.

After graduating I accepted an offer to work at Samsung's India mobile R&D division. SRI-B.

Here, I have been working for last three years, primarily on connectivity module of Samsung devices, specialising in Bluetooth.

I love to travel, run, play and write, among other hobbies.
