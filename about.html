About Me

I am from Begusarai. Not the town. But a village in that district, some 40-50km away from that district town. In the eastern part of India (it's the East, not North; never). That's where I finished primary school.

For high school I moved to a local boarding school near another town Rosera. I spent few good years there.

I did my senior secondary at Chinmaya Vidyalaya Bokaro.

Then I moved to NIT Patna for undergraduate studies in the field of Computer Science.

After graduating I moved to Bangalore and worked at Samsung's India mobile R&D division SRI-B for a few years. Then at Ola Cabs. After that I worked at Socure. Domain wise, over the years, it's been all over (it's kinda fun, building things in a new domain) but tech has been Android and mobile pretty much throughout.
